Bruce House & Rose House

2 Bedroom Apartment
Sleeps 4

These apartments are situated directly opposite Weighbridge Cottage. This offers larger groups the option to book several properties and be within 2 minutes of each other.

Each apartment has a double bedroom and a twin bedroom.

Bruce House & Rose House sit three miles west of Aberdeen City Centre, Train Station and Ferry Terminal, and six miles from Aberdeen Airport. A bus stop serving the city centre and other destinations are within a few minutes walk from the apartments, with buses every 15 minutes.

Within walking distance is Hazelhead Golf Courses and Hazelhead Park. There is also a cycle route towards Westhill, beginning four minutes from the apartments.

A convenience store is located across the road about five minutes walk away. There is a supermarket less than 10 minutes walk or 5 minutes by car.
