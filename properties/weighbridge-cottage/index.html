Weighbridge Cottage

2 Bedroom Cottage
Sleeps 6

Weighbridge Cottage is a 2 bedroom granite cottage situated in the West End of Aberdeen.

It sits three miles west of Aberdeen City Centre, Train Station and Ferry Terminal. Aberdeen Airport is six miles from the cottage. A bus stop serving the city centre and other destinations are within few minutes walk from the cottage, with buses every 15 minutes.

Within walking distance is Hazelhead Golf Courses and Hazelhead Park. There is also a cycle route towards Westhill, beginning four minutes from the cottage.

A convenience store is located across the road about five minutes walk away. There is a supermarket less than 10 minutes walk or 5 minutes by car.

Cottage Features

  • Sleeps 6

    The cottage has a double bedroom and twin room with a double fold out bed in living room.

  • Modern Interior

    The cottage is decorated with modern, light and airy furniture and fittings.

  • Private Garden

    There is a secure, secluded garden to the rear of property with a drying line if required.

  • Parking Available

    Off road parking for two car is available at front of property.

  • Welcome Pack

    A welcome self catering pack of tea, coffee, milk and biscuits on arrival
